What naki is

A small, honest way to stay connected to the people around you.

No noisy feeds. No vanity numbers. Just a clear path to a real comrade — and an easy way to offer or ask for what you have to share.

Connect with a comrade

Match with people close by who share your routine, your goals, or your street — and start a conversation that actually leads somewhere.

Share a resource

Post a tool, a skill, a spare seat, a meal — whatever you can offer today. Naki App makes the offer visible to comrades who need it.

Check in, often

A light nudge to ask how a comrade is doing — because community isn't a one-time match, it's something you keep tending.
